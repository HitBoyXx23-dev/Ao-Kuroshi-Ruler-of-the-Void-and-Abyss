⚫ AO KUROSHI

The Ruler of the Void and Abyss

Name

Ao Kuroshi (蒼黒死) — Blue-Black Death

Titles

  • The Ruler of the Void and Abyss
  • The Pale Monarch
  • The Silent Flame
  • He Who Stills the Heavens

Divine Colors & Symbols

  • Black: Infinite absence, the womb of silence.
  • Dark Blue: The depth of unseen truth.
  • Purple: The color of extinguished divinity, royal and mournful.
  • Accent: A faint blue-white flame flickering downward.
  • A circle of black ink with three violet dots — The Abyss Crown
  • The “Falling Flame” — a droplet-shaped blue fire pointing down
  • The Kuroshi Spiral — drawn in one motion, symbol of eternal descent

🜂 The Subordinates of Ao Kuroshi

Nemura, the Veiled Dawn — Herald of Silence. Blindfolded goddess; one word per century, suns die. Symbol: Blind eye wreathed in blue flame.

Zeroth, the Inward King — Keeper of Hollow Thrones. Empty armor beside the throne. Symbol: Inverted crown.

Shi’en, the Blue Maw — Devourer of Names. The unseen swimmer of the Abyss. Symbol: Three ash circles.

Kagero, the Whispering Flame — Spirit of Lost Voices. Candle for the unheard dead. Symbol: Downward flame between silence marks (—).

⚫ Worship & Rituals

Temples lie beneath mountains, lit by blue flames that never rise. Robes: black silk with indigo lining, rings of violet glass. Prayer: whispered on the last breath —

“In silence, I return.
In shadow, I am seen.
In the flame that falls, I am unmade.
Ao Kuroshi, breathe through me —
Until nothing remains.”
  • Rite of the Ink Mirror: Sins spoken to dissolve reflection.
  • Offering of Sound: A single note fades to silence.
  • Falling Flame Ceremony: Blue lantern descends until extinguished.

🜄 The Abyssal Creed

“All sound must end.”
“Light betrays truth — shadow remembers.”
“Do not pray to be seen. Pray to vanish rightly.”
